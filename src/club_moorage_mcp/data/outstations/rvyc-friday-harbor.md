In the Port of Friday Harbor Marina on San Juan Island, USA. RVYC leases one 40' slip
— C69, at the outside end of C Dock. Moorage and power are free to RVYC members and
fresh water is available at the slip. Boats over 50' OAL are not permitted on the
dock, but anchoring in the harbour (good holding) is an option; there are no mooring
balls. Rafting up to three wide is supported via 'Y' pigtails at the leased slip's
electrical outlet — use only the designated outlets.

Marina amenities include washrooms, laundry, and garbage disposal; holding-tank pump
outs are available on the same terms as for other Port customers.

Upon arrival, record each night's stay in the RVYC registration book at the
Harbormaster's Office. If the office is closed, complete the 'late arrivals' envelope
at the entrance with vessel name, owner, dates, and slip C69, and place it in the
designated slot. Abide by all Port Authority marina rules. Checkout is 1200.
