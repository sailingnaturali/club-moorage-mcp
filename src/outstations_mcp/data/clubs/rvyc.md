General outstation rules for all RVYC members:

- Stays are limited to three consecutive nights unless space is available and the
  RVYC office has been advised.
- Checkout time is 1200.
- Quiet hours are 2200 to 0800.
- All boats shall fly the RVYC burgee.
- The overboard discharge of sewage or bilge pumping is prohibited.
- Boats may not be left unattended overnight unless approved by the General Manager.
- Boats shall be rafted where there is no dock space; larger vessels lie alongside
  the dock with smaller vessels rafted to them. Members not using empty space may
  find their vessel moved and re-tied by another member needing room.
- There are no reciprocal privileges with other yacht clubs at RVYC outstations.
- Booking an outstation reserves general usage of the facility, not a specific berth;
  cancel a booking you cannot use.
