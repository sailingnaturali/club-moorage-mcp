Near the end of the protected waters of Long Harbour on Salt Spring Island, on the
west side by the launch ramp — about 0.8 nm past the BC Ferries terminal. Dock space
is limited; there are also two mooring buoys and anchoring space. Boats over 55' OAL
must use a mooring buoy rather than the dock.

Onshore: onsite caretaker, fully-equipped kitchen, toilet and shower, inside dining
area, outdoor deck and BBQ, patio seating, child and adult bicycles, kayaks and
sailing dinghies, a sandy beach with swimming, and 15/30 amp power at the docks (no
charge). An independent shuttle driver serves Ganges. Fresh water is in short supply
and must be conserved. Long Harbour is a pack-in/pack-out facility — no garbage is
left. Upon arrival sign the guest book and record the expected number of nights.

Getting around: a taxi (Silver Shadow) and the local bus (fare $2.25) are available.
By road the outstation is at 105 Ontario Place, at the end of Ontario Place by the
water.
