Telegraph Harbour Marina on Thetis Island — a popular stopover in the heart of the
Gulf Islands, with kayaking, paddleboarding, cycling, island B&Bs, and a short ferry
ride to Chemainus. RVYC has a standing agreement with the marina for member outstation
privileges.

Conditions: RVYC has 80 feet per day of reserved dock space year-round, excluding the
May and September long weekends, available first-come, first-served. If all reserved
Club space is full, members receive a 15 percent discount on standard moorage rates.
Reserved Club moorage is not available during scheduled rendezvous events.

Booking differs from the other outstations: book at telegraphharbour.com — click
Reservations and Rates, then Individual Bookings; enter arrival and departure dates
and Book; complete the vessel information form and select Royal Victoria Yacht Club
from the Outstation Yacht Club list. An email confirmation from the marina is required
before arrival. On arrival, check in at the marina office, identify as an RVYC member,
display the reservation card as instructed, and fly the RVYC burgee.
